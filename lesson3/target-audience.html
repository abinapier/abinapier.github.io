Target Audience

The target audience for this website is young to middle aged adults who are familiar with technology. They are students or in the working class. Their income ranges from low to middle-high class. They are people who want a clean, simple, and modern way to recieve their weather information quickly and effectively.

Personas

Persona 1:

Group Student
Job/Responsibilities Attends college, works on campus
Demographics
  • 20
  • Single
  • Some college
  • Low
Goals She is organized and motivated. She wants to do well in school and be succesful at her job. She spends her time studing, working, and spending time with friends.
Enviornment She is very comfortable with technology. The internet at her apartment can be slower at times, but on campus it is reliable and fast. When she is outside she uses her cellular data on her phone to access the internet, which is reliable. She accesses the internet on her phone and laptop.

Persona 2:

Group Worker
Job/Responsibilities Software Engineer
Demographics
  • 32
  • Married with no children
  • Masters Degree
  • Middle to High
Goals He is a hard worker and works long hours to get projects done at work. He enjoys his job. He is also active and spends his time off work doing outdoor activities with his wife.
Enviornment He is very comfortable with technology. The internet at his job and his home are both fast and reliable. During his commutes he uses cellular data. He accesses the internet on his phone, laptop, and work desktop.

Scenarios

  1. How cold is it going to be while I walk to school today?
  2. Will the weather be bad enough in the morning that I'll need to schedule extra time to get to work?
  3. Is next weekend's weather going to be warm enough for a backpacking trip?
  4. How warm is it where my family is?
  5. Will it warm up enough today that I need to wear layers that can be removed thoughout the day?
