Site Name:

Oh, Weather

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide local weather to users in a way that is easy to understand and nice to look at. The site should provide accurate current weather conditions and show in an organized way the future weather conditions. The site should work equally well on any size screen and should load quickly. It should be easy to use, especially for first time users.
