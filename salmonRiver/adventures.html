Our Adventures:

Day Trip

learn more

Our day trip adventure is perfect for beginners. We spend the first part of the day training on saftey and tecnique. Following the lunch that we provide, we'll guide you through exciting rapids.

Length:

8 hours

Skill Level:

beginner

Cost:

$50 per person

What we Do:

  • saftey training
  • provide gear
  • provide lunch

Weekend Trip

learn more

Our weekend trip typically starts on a Thursday, but can be arranged to start on any day. We spend the first half of the first day going over basic saftey and training rules, as well as fitting and checking gear. After lunch we start out on the water. We camp overnight for two nights, and end our trip mid-day on the third day. The rapids are suitable for an intermediate rafter.

Length:

3 days

Skill Level:

intermediate

Cost:

$150 per person

What we Do:

  • guide to campsites
  • provide all gear
  • provide all meals

Week Trip

learn more

This is for those who love rafting as much as we do. We do the planning, you'll do the rafting. This trip is for advanced rafters, so we provide the gear, the route and the food and you provide the experience and rafting. You'll still have a guide with you to make sure everything goes well, but otherwise we just take care of the less fun parts of your trip.

Length:

5 days

Skill Level:

advanced

Cost:

$250 per person

What we Do:

  • rafting route
  • provide all gear
  • provide all meals
